Gurukul for Mind and Body

Gurukul for Mind, Body, and Spirit offers a traditional Indian education system that focuses on holistic wellness of an individual. This system integrates yoga, meditation, and other practices that promote mental, physical, and spiritual well-being. Join us at 79 Longitude Yoga to experience this enriching way of learning.

About us

79 Longitude Yoga offers a range of yoga classes designed to promote physical and mental well-being. Our experienced instructors guide students through each practice, providing modifications and adjustments to ensure that every student feels supported and comfortable. We offer classes for all levels of experience, from beginners to advanced practitioners, and focus on building strength, flexibility, and balance. Our studio is a welcoming and inclusive space where students can connect with themselves and others, and find a sense of community.
